Auctions

TRON AUCTION has three auctions with different sizes of bets: 20, 100 and 1000 coins, and timers: 1 minute, 2 minutes, 15 minutes accordingly. You are able to play in one or several auctions at the same time.

How to play

To start playing in Auction, you need to bet. When the timer ends, the auction leader receives the entire Bank from this auction.

Initial Bank

Initially, the auction Bank is 50% of the bet. That is, it is equal to: 20, 100, and 1000 coins, respectively.

First bet

When the player has placed the first bet in Auction: - this player becomes the auction leader, - the auction timer starts, - this bet is added to the current Bank.

In the case when there was only one single bet, at the end of the timer the prize will be 150% of the bet.

Secondary bets

As long as the timer counts down, any player (except the current leader) can also bet. In this case: - he becomes the auction leader, - the auction timer restarts, - 70% of his bet adds to the new current Bank.

Wallet Delay

Remember that the wallet does not process the request immediately, it takes a few seconds. In the last seconds of the timer, the wallet may not have time to process your bet. In case when the auction wallet hasn't accepted the bet due to the lack of time, then it will not be withdrawn from your wallet, but you will become the leader of the Auction.

Wallet Commission

TRON wallet takes from each bet a commission of approximately 0.3 coins.
